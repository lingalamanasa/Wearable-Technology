Our Story

Redefining How Humans Connect with Technology

Founded in 2022, Stackly began with a simple yet ambitious vision: to create wearable technology that genuinely improves people's lives. Our founders, a team of biomedical engineers and AI researchers, saw the untapped potential in combining advanced sensors with machine learning.

Today, Stackly is at the forefront of the wearable revolution, with devices used by over 120,000 people across 40+ countries. From detecting early signs of heart disease to enabling hands-free AR experiences in enterprise settings, our technology is making a real difference.

Our R&D lab in San Francisco houses over 60 scientists and engineers working on next-generation biosensors, energy harvesting, and spatial computing — technologies that will define the next decade of wearable innovation.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

2022

Stackly Founded

Three biomedical engineers and an AI researcher launch Stackly in a San Francisco garage with a vision to revolutionize wearable health tech.

2023

First Product Launch

Stackly Watch v1 ships with continuous heart rate monitoring and AI-powered health insights. Sells out in 48 hours.

2024

Series B & AR Expansion

Raises $50M Series B. Launches AR glasses division and acquires BioSense Labs for advanced sensor technology.

2025

Global Expansion

Opens offices in London, Tokyo, and Mumbai. Reaches 100K+ active users. Launches Developer SDK and partner program.

2026

Next-Gen Platform

Unveils Stackly Vision Pro AR glasses and the AI Health Engine 3.0. Named "Most Innovative Wearable Company" by TechCrunch.
